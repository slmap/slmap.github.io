Geopolitics of South Caucasus

A Region of Permanent Struggle

  • Geopolitics will remain the main dividing factor preventing states of the South Caucasus region from building viable cooperation.
  • Powerful states neighbouring the region will continue exercising their influence playing on disagreements of the regional nations. However, due to the nature of the regional geopolitics neither of the big powers will be able to construct long lasting alignments or take a leadership role in the region.
South Caucasus is a complicated region. For twenty seven years since the disintegration of the Soviet Union the region has not dusted off its main problem the one of mutual hostility and vulnerability that weaken it placing subject to the influence of its powerful neighbours.
